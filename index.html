About Us

Leading Manufactures & Exporters Of Paper Sticks (Lolipop Paper Sticks), Colored Paper Sticks, Printed Paper Sticks

Eco Paper Stick Products is a leading company in India that produces and supplies a wide range of paper products for various industries.

Established in Gujarat, India in 2018 as a partnership firm by Mr. Ashokbhai Bhanderi, Mr. Raj Poshiya, and Mr. Raj Kamdiya, Eco Paper Stick Products specializes in manufacturing paper sticks, including lollipop paper sticks, colored paper sticks, and printed paper sticks. Within 10 years, the company significantly expanded its production lines to capture both Indian and overseas markets. Eco Paper Stick Products is dedicated to producing high-quality, environmentally friendly paper products using the latest technology and modern machinery to ensure consistency in quality and dimensional accuracy.
